Blog post one

Posted by Fahad on march 10

Cristiano Ronaldo Dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese proffessional footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus and captains the portugal national team.He borns in 1985 in a small island of Portugal name Madeira. He was very passionate about football from very early age. At the age of 9 he was selected in Sporting CP accademy for his talents. To join the accademy he left his home at just 9. He was promoted to Sporting CP main team at only 16 years old. Then he got some attention from World wide. Sir Alex Farguson, Boss of English club Manchester United saw something in 17 years old Cristiano. He signed him for United. After joining United Cristiano never had to look back.
